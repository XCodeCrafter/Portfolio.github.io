At our company, I handle programming for various machinery, using systems Sinumeric D840, Heidenhain, and Fusion 360. I'm in charge of setting up tools, designing fixtures for products, testing and fine-tuning programs until I get the final part just right. I measure everything and we send the first part for approval to our customer. Products are for the automotive industry, high-quality standards and demanding tolerances. I am programming with R and Q parameters on Sinumeric and Heidenhain. Same level of skill on both systems. Programming for 5 axis machines. Parts till 40kg, from steel, cast steel, and aluminium parts till 2kg.
